# Company Business

Yuanta Securities is a leading financial services company in Thailand, providing a broad range of investment and securities solutions. Its offerings include equities, derivatives, bonds, mutual funds, structured products, and investment advisory services for both retail and institutional clients. The company operates a comprehensive trading and investment platform supported by front-office, middle-office, and back-office systems to ensure seamless execution, settlement, and risk management.

# Data Design Principles
## Naming Convention
Fields ending with Id store an integer that references a master record.
Fields storing constants always reference a constant list by constant type.

## Company Master Data
Service Category: List of service group that yuanta has, e.g. porfolio advisory, equity (sbl), tfex (block trade)
SubscribeLevel : Refer to service subscription level, in Yuanta there 3 layer including person, customer code and account code
Services: List of company services, e.g., SBL service, Block Trade service.
## Constant Master Data
Constant Type: Defines a key for a set of constants, e.g., marital status, gender, education level.
Constant List: Enumeration of values for each constant type, e.g., for gender → 'male', 'female', 'other'.
## Customer Profile
Customer: Main table used as a reference for other customer-related data.
Basic Profile: Stores personal information strictly about the individual, independent of company data.
Address: Stores customer addresses; a customer may have multiple addresses.
Contact Information: Stores customer contact details.
ServiceRequest: Relation between customer and service with approval status
